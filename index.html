Hi, I'm Alejandra!

I am a freelance UX/UI designer based out of Northern Virginia.
I specialize in User Research and Analysis and enjoy solving user problems that focus on making everyday technology more accessible and enjoyable for all.
My skills include User Interviews, Usability Tests, Project Management, and Human-Centered Thinking and I am proficient in Figma, Google Workspace, and Miro.
The art of creation is my passion and when I’m not wireframing or building personas to encapsulate user needs, I can be found writing my next short story or learning a new art medium.

Works

National Park Service Redesign

The National Park Service website was updated to have a more intuitive design and a higher performing usability, but was given a vintage-looking design that calls back to the its historic roots.

Chicago Reader Redesign

Current consumption of online news has decreased in the age of social media, so the design team brought a social media approach to Chicago’s alternative newsroom.

Fridge Friends Mobile App

From taking stock of the fridge to offering substitutes when an ingredient isn’t available, Fridge Friends is a mobile app that aims to simplify the cooking process. The project is a mid-fidelity prototype.

Secretary Mobile App

Secretary is a mobile app that allows digital nomads to find activities in their city that fits their unique schedule– from the ultra famous sites to the niche events. The project is a mid-fidelity prototype.
